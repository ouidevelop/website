Software Solutions

We recently built a security system for a large news organization. The software scans through the code they have pushed to GitHub looking for exposed credentials. If it catches credentials, it automatically invalidates them and emails the security team as well as the person who made the commit.

If you, or your company is interested in implementing such an application, feel free to contact us!

Team

  1. Marine is a full stack engineer and specializes in JavaScript. Her experience includes designing UI/UX with frameworks and libraries such as AngularJS and d3.js, architecting RESTful APIs with technologies such as Node.js and Express, and utilizing both relational and non-relational databases. She loves to employ her creativity and technical skills to develop ethical software and build elegant UI and data visualizations. Most recently, she collaborated with engineers to improve the online discussion of news articles by leading the frontend development of Fiskkit . Previously, as a UC Berkeley visiting researcher in linguistics and cognitive science, Marine designed and coded multiple behavioral experiments.

  2. Michael is a full stack engineer who specializes in Go and JavaScript. Before starting Oui Develop, he worked as a software engineer at General Electric. He has experience with Node.js and Go on the backend, and AngularJS and Polymer on the frontend. He also worked using relational databases and non relational databases such as MongoDB, Postgres, Amazon S3 and Redis. He especially enjoys solving software architectural problems and cares about thoroughly testing his code.
